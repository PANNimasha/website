Chicken Fried Rice

INGREDIENTS : 500g basmati or samba rice, cooked 250g boneless chicken, cut into small cubes 50g carrot, julienned 50g leeks, julienned (green part) 50g leek stalk, julienned 3 eggs, beaten 1 onion, chopped 2 garlic cloves, chopped 1 inch ginger, chopped 20g Astra Salt and pepper to taste
METHOD : Melt Astra in a pan and add onion, garlic and ginger. Saut√© till fragrant. Scramble the eggs in a separate pan and set aside. Add chicken, a pinch of salt and pepper and cook for a few minutes. Add carrot, leeks, leek stalk. Quickly stir-fry the vegetables for 2 minutes. Add cooked rice and scrambled eggs. Mix well and serve hot. This scrumptious quick recipe is easy to make and delicious to serve!
