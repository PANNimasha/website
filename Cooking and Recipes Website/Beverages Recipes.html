PineApple Juice

INGREDIENTS : 1 fully ripe pineapple. 1 can evaporated milk . 1/2 cup sugar. 2 -3 tsp vanilla essence. 4-5 cups ice cubes (if you prefer less sweet you can add more ice).
METHOD : Peel and cut up pineapple into cubes. Put into a blender. Add evaporated milk, sugar and Ice cubes and blend it. Add vanilla last and blend in. Pouring into a glass container then Put in refrigerator for a while (about 30 minutes) serve cold.
